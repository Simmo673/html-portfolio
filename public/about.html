About Me

Tom Simmonds started life as a professional musician before illness forced him to change direction.

Whilst he was coming to terms with his new life he started drawing. He found that he had a real talent for art and progressed very quickly, soon getting commissions for his work

His wife, Deborah Firmstone, owns a successful marketing and events company, Queen Bee & Co. Due to the expansion of the business, Tom has also retrained as a web developer, in order to offer websites to Deborah's clients.
